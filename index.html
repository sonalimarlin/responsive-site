Recent Travels

Truck Problems: Ladghar India

Heading to Ladghar, south of Mumbai, we spied a truck heading up an incline, with a off-balanced load perched precariously on the road! The driver was nowhere to be found. Presumably gone to look for some help!?

Immense Horizons: South Park CO

The 10,000 foot high prairie called South Park in Colorado frequently gets some pretty amazing sunsets, and cloud formations. The play of sunlight and shadow with dry scrubby hills makes for some spectacular photos!

Lake Reflections: Louisville CO

Pretty little Harper Lake in Louisville doesn't lack for pretty sunset colors either! This one shows the skies ablaze in color while the front range mountains stand guard on the horizon.

Dachau Entrance: Munich Germany

The somber entrance to the Dachau Concentration camp, located just outside of Munich Germany. It's hard to imagine the peaceful quiet place as it stands now, as a place of such misery and suffering. But there's also such a strong story of strength and resilience when you read the stories of the prisoners.

Town Center: Munich Germany

Cute buildings surround Munich's central square, filled with street buskers, tourists, locals hustling back and forth from work, and plenty of cheap yummy street food and quaint cafes.

Wind Palace: Jaipur India

Jaipur India, busy and full of activity with streets lined with vendors, roads full to bursting with people, motorcycles, cycle rickshaws, cars, and trucks. And beautiful old red sandstone palaces and old havelis (palatial residences).
